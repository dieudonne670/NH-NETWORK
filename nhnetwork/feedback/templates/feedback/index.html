    • Welcome to NH-NETWORK

    NH-network is web-application platform to enhance network performance monitoring and user satisfaction for mobile subscribers of providers such as MTN, Orange, and others. The app will track metrics like signal strength and internet speed, and gather feedback on your network experience. Your input is critical to defining features that meet your needs. This survey takes approximately 2-3 minutes to complete. Thank you for contributing to a better mobile experience!
